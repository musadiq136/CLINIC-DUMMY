Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our clinic and treatments.

What are your consultation fees?

Our initial consultation fee typically ranges between PKR 2,500 and PKR 3,500. This includes a thorough examination and discussion of your dental goals.

What are your business hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday, from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays to allow our staff to rest and recharge.

Where is the clinic located?

KH Dental & Aesthetics Clinic is located at No. G, Yousuf Grand Plot, M-25, Mezzanine Floor, 8 KDA Scheme 5, Block 8 Clifton, Karachi, 74200.

Do you offer cosmetic dentistry?

Yes, we specialize in high-end cosmetic dentistry, including veneers, teeth whitening, smile enhancement, and complete smile makeovers.
